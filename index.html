Our Story

Crafted for the Discerning Client

Quinline Mobile Salon was born from a partnership between two passionate stylists — Quin and Alexine — united by one belief: premium hair care should come to you, not the other way around. Based in Biyam Assi, Yaoundé, we deliver salon-grade services directly to your home, office, or event.

Together, we bring a unique blend of precision barbering and creative styling. Our duo is trained in the latest techniques, equipped with professional-grade tools, and committed to the highest standards of hygiene.

Whether it's a flawless barb, luxurious braids, or a full color transformation — we arrive prepared, we work as a team, and we leave you looking extraordinary.
